Welcome to our coffee bar(n)

With every sip, one thing is clear, we want to create a welcoming, farm feel space and fill it with love, connections, community, giggles (lots of giggles) and top it off with beautiful and excellent beverages for everyone.


Experience

sweet delight

We work closely with our roasting partners, a BC local roaster to source our signature Coop-a-cino Blends. We also have a rotating lineup of incredible brand sometimes award-winning single origin and blended coffees from all over the world. Our coffees are carefully sourced and master roasted batch by batch for best consistency.   The best of the best. To top it off, a portion of the proceeds from every purchase of our coffees, will help our local communities and the ones of the coffee farmers, a project we proudly support our through our roaster. It is really something you taste with every sip and will make you feel good.
